Introduction

SaigonTech library is open to all SaigonTech members including current instructors, registered students and current staff. It has a collection of more than 5000 printing titles which focus on Information Technology, Economics, Mathematics, sociology, English, etc in the form of originals, photocopies, magazines, e-books and media, etc.

Vision

SaigonTech library is a modern Library and Information center, flexible in meeting diverse information needs of school members through professional staffs who good at organizing, providing and supporting exploiting information resources.

Mission statement

Supporting learning, teaching and researching of school by acquisition high - quality information resources, providing the best service (if possible) in order to promote using and exploiting information for SaigonTech instructors, students and staffs effectively:

  • Choosing, acquisition information resources with different formats, classifying, organizing storage, preserving, using instructions, ... supporting learning needs which is suitable to school mission and programs.
  • Equipping suitable facilities, equipment for exploiting information resources.
  • Providing the access right, consulting services and training for information users in exploiting information effectively
  • Providing some basic information exploitation skills to meet needs of self-search information, encouraging lifelong learning, helping improve quality of life.
  • Cooperating with other libraries to enrich information resources and services that library information users can access.

Regulations

  • Show your card upon request. Student card, instructor card or staff card is Library card also. Visitors not affiliated with the school must have an introduction letter from their office to use the services of the Library.
  • Be responsible for your possessions. The Library accepts no responsibility for loss, theft of, or damage of personal belongings.
  • Please observe the Library’s norms. Please be respectful by being silent and keeping the Library clean. All behaviour which disturb other users is not allowed.
  • Do not smoke or bring food, drink into the Library. Anyone found with food or drink will be asked to remove it. Drink water at the designated areas.
  • Please preserve Library materials. Users are forbidden to substitute fraudulently, appropriate, mutilate, tear, write in, throw, lose, or cause other damages to materials belonging to the Library.
  • Absolutely do not carry materials out of the Library without permission from Library staff.
  • Do not move tables, chairs, computers and other facilities of the Library.
  • Comply with the rules of school. SaigonTech prohibits the violation of Vietnamese law.
  • Depending on the violation, violator may be punished with warning, being asked to leave the Library, fined, suspended from the library, or expelled from the school.
  • We stop serving 15 minutes before closing time.
